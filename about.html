Inequality, Equality, Equity, and Justice

It is essential to understand the differences between Inequality, Equality, Equity, and Justice.

  • Inequality: Unequal access to opportunities
  • Equality: Evenly distributed tools and assistance
  • Equity: Custom tools that identify and address inequality
  • Justice: Fixing the system to offer equal access to both tools and opportunities

Significance

The visual representations and definitions of these concepts helps solidfy our understanding of how to work toward the overarching goal of justice in all systems to which we, as human beings, have access.
